About Us

Metaria ry is a non-profit association founded in 2015 by Tuomo Karvonen, Matti Luhtala, and Jyrki Pylväs. Metaria promotes and explores new forms of performing arts in digital environments. Showcasing innovative tech-driven performance art nationally and internationally.

Digital Support

Digital tools are a natural part of how Metaria works, and we aim to make it easy for everyone to participate in what we do. Through our digital guidance service, we provide practical support for the platforms and workflows we use, ensuring that communication, collaboration, and project activities run seamlessly. You’re welcome to reach out by sending a request through the form below or simply calling us, and the support is provided free of charge.
