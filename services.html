Tension & Trauma Releasing Exercises (TRE®)

Tension & Trauma Releasing Exercises (TRE) is a body-based method designed to activate natural neurogenic tremors that help release deep muscular tension and support nervous system regulation. During the session, participants are guided through a sequence of simple exercises followed by a controlled tremor response, allowing the body to discharge accumulated stress in a safe and structured way.

  • Reduction of chronic muscular tension and physical stress
  • Support for recovery from work-related and emotional stress
  • Improved relaxation, sleep quality, and overall wellbeing

Building the Stage

Gautam Vishwanath guides you through the three-day workshop to explore the creative and strategic potential of extended reality (XR) — including VR, AR, MR, AI, and gamification — for storytelling, design, and innovation. Participants move from inspiration to hands-on prototyping using no-code tools and XR headsets, developing immersive concepts tailored to their goals. The final session focuses on refining prototypes and creating a clear six-month roadmap for continued growth and implementation.
